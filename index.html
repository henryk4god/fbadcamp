Which Guide Should You Use?

Choose Andromeda Update if:

  • You're selling digital products
  • You want a simpler, faster setup
  • You trust Meta's AI optimization
  • You can create multiple ad variations
  • You're comfortable with CBO budgeting
  • You want to leverage modern Meta algorithms

Choose Complete Beginner's Guide if:

  • This is your first Facebook Ads campaign
  • You're selling physical products or services
  • You want full control over audience targeting
  • You prefer testing audiences before scaling
  • You want to understand ABO vs CBO deeply
  • You need step-by-step hand-holding
